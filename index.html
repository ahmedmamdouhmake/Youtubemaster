Instructions for Adding the Cydia Repository:

To add the Cydia repository, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Cydia application on your device.
  2. Tap on 'Sources' in the bottom menu.
  3. Tap on 'Edit' and then 'Add'.
  4. Enter the URL of the repository: [Repository URL]
  5. Tap 'Add Source' and confirm.
